# PharmaGuard AI
## Business Goal 

PharmaGuard AI is a privacy-first platform designed to detect fraudulent prescription patterns in real time. Pharmaceutical companies and health insurers lose billions annually due to undetected fraud such as abnormal prescription volumes, off-label drug usage, and reimbursement manipulation.

Current detection systems rely on delayed manual audits, often identifying issues months after they occur. PharmaGuard AI addresses this gap by enabling proactive, automated, and explainable fraud detection.
